# EsmaeelE

**Systems Engineer & Free Software Enthusiast**

I build and maintain infrastructure that runs quietly in the background — the kind that just works. Over the past 8+ years I have worked across the full infrastructure stack, from bare-metal servers to Kubernetes clusters in the cloud. My focus is on automation, reliability, and keeping operations clean and repeatable.

I believe in open-source software, simple tooling, and writing things down so they can be shared.

---

## What I Work On

- Building and maintaining **CI/CD pipelines** that ship code safely
- Operating **Kubernetes clusters** and container workloads at scale
- Managing **infrastructure as code** with Terraform and Ansible
- Setting up **monitoring and observability** with Prometheus, Grafana, and the ELK stack
- Automating repetitive tasks — if it can be scripted, it should be
